Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Click on any question to expand.

+What is Zuzz.tv?
Zuzz.tv is a premium sports streaming platform that offers live access to all major sports including NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, UFC, Soccer, Tennis, and more. We provide HD quality streams without blackout restrictions, so you never miss a game.
+Which countries is your service available to?
Our service is available worldwide! Whether you're in the USA, Europe, Asia, or anywhere else, you can access Zuzz.tv with any internet connection. No geo-restrictions.
+Is there a dedicated app for your service?
Yes! We have a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on iOS, Android, and all smart devices. Simply visit our website on your phone and add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.
+Do you offer returns or refunds?
Absolutely! We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, contact support within 7 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
+What type of support can I expect?
We offer 24/7 customer support via email and live chat. Our dedicated team typically responds within minutes and is always ready to help with any issues.
+Do you have blackout restrictions?
NO! Unlike cable TV and official league passes, we have ZERO blackout restrictions. Watch any game, any team, from anywhere in the world.
+What sports do you offer?
We cover all major sports: NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NCAA Football & Basketball, UFC/MMA, Boxing, Soccer (EPL, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Champions League), Tennis, Golf, F1, and much more!
+What are the acceptable payment methods?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and various other payment methods. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
+Which browsers do you recommend?
For the best experience, we recommend Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Our player is optimized for all modern browsers and works great on both desktop and mobile.
+Do you have auto-billing?
No, our subscriptions do NOT auto-renew. You're in complete control - when your subscription ends, you simply renew if you want to continue.
+Can I cancel anytime?
Since we don't have auto-billing, there's nothing to cancel! Your subscription simply expires at the end of your chosen period.
+How many devices are allowed at the same time?
Depending on your plan: 2-Day and Weekly passes allow 1 device, Monthly allows 2 devices, and Annual allows up to 3 devices streaming simultaneously.
+Do you have DVR service?
Currently, we don't offer DVR. However, most events have replays available within 24 hours after the live broadcast ends.
+How can you offer subscriptions at such a low price?
We use efficient streaming technology, have minimal overhead, and believe sports should be accessible to everyone. We cut out the middlemen to bring you the best value.
+Is Zuzz.tv legal?
We operate as a streaming platform providing access to sports content. Users are responsible for complying with their local laws regarding streaming services.
+Are Pay-Per-View (PPV) events included?
YES! All PPV events including UFC fights, Boxing matches, and wrestling events are included in your subscription at no extra cost.
+Is Zuzz.tv blocked in my country?
Zuzz.tv works in most countries. If you experience any access issues, try using a VPN or contact our support team for assistance.
